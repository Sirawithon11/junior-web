What is ICPC

ICPC Thailand National Competition

The International Collegiate Programming Contest is a prestigious algorithmic competition for college students. Teams of three from universities solve real-world problems, enhancing collaboration, creativity, and pressure management. It is the oldest, largest, and most renowned programming contest globally.
